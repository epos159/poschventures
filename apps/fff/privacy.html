Privacy Policy

Faith Focus Friend · Last updated: February 2026

Summary

Faith Focus Friend is free, works offline, and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. No ads. No tracking. The only permission we request is photo/library access, and only for saving drawings to your device.

Information We Collect

None. We do not collect personal data from users. The app does not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or any third-party data collection.

Data Storage

Words, drawings, session results, and settings are stored only on your device. We do not have access to this data. It never leaves your device unless you share it (for example, by exporting or backing up your phone).

Photo / Media Library Access

If you choose to save a drawing, the app may request access to your photo library (or media library) so we can save the drawing image to your device. We use this permission only for that purpose. We do not upload, read, or access any of your other photos or media. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.

Children

Faith Focus Friend is intended for use by families. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. The app is designed so that no data is sent to us.

App Stores

When you download the app from the Apple App Store or Google Play, Apple or Google may collect account and device information as part of their services. That is governed by their respective privacy policies, not ours.

Optional Donations

If we add an optional donation feature in the future, payments will be processed by a third-party provider. We would not store payment details. The provider's privacy policy would govern transaction data.

Changes

We may update this policy if the app changes. The "Last updated" date above will reflect any changes.

Contact

Questions? Contact us at support@poschventures.com.
